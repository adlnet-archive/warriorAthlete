About This Course

You can earn six badges in this challenge: Ethos, Mobility, Strength, Endurance, and Nutrition, plus one overall Warrior Athlete competency badge. For each badge, you need to take three steps:

  1. Learn about what it takes to be a Warrior Athlete in each badge area.
  2. Plan steps you will take to achieve the badge.
  3. Execute the plan!

The challenge will take a minimum of 30 days of daily workouts and nutritional discipline to complete.
