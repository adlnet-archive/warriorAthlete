Warrior Athlete Nutrition Badge  

Make the right choices with the Nutrition Mission Game

Instructions: In a similar manner as the previous Nutrition Mission game type, you will be placed in a virtual world DFAC line in order to apply your new knowledge about proper nutrition choices. Different than before, this time you will start with an emply tray and will have to make choices about which foods to pick for a healthy meal. 

To help you learn about proper standards of nutrition, you will need to keep within the calorie goal set in the game and also meet the appropriate proportions of protein, fat, and carbohydrates neccessary for a well balanced meal that keeps you at the top of your game.
