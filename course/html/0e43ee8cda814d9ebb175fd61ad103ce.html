The Warrior Athlete Challenge is a step-by-step, highly achievable program for you to get fit and healthy. It emphasizes particular aspects of physical health that are designed to help you in your military operational readiness and career.

This challenge is highly self-directed. You need to be on your game in terms of honestly assessing your strengths, weaknesses, and progress towards goals.

There are four phases of activities for all badges except the Ethos badge: 1) Learn 2) Plan 3) Execute 4) Discuss. The Ethos badge only has the Learn and Discuss phases. These phases should be done in that order, but the activities required within each phase can be done in any order. These activities are:

1) Learn

  • Watch videos (all badges)
  • Read character blogs (Nutrition badge)
  • Play and pass game (Nutrition badge)
  • Pass a quiz (all badges)

2) Plan

  • Create Nutrition plan (Nutrition badge)
  • Create Physical Readiness Training (PRT) Plan (Endurance, Mobility, Strength badges)
  • Create a social network for supporting progress (Endurance, Mobility, Strength, and Nutrition badges)

3) Execute

  • Enter daily log of progress towards plan goals. (Endurance, Mobility, Strength, and Nutrition badges)
  • Use social network established in Plan phase to support progress (Endurance, Mobility, Strength, and Nutrition badges)

4) Discuss

  • Participate in a discussion with other Warrior Athlete students about activities and resources related to this badge (all badges)

Depending on the number of badges you achieve, you will earn Beginner (1-2 badges), Intermediate (3-4) badges, or Advanced (all 5 badges) Warrior Athlete status.

You can work on the badges in any order, and return to them at any time. They are available as the course sections shown in the left panel.

Start working on a badge when you are ready.
