Warrior Athlete Endurance Badge  

Create Physical Readiness Training (PRT) Plan

Instructions: Using what you have learned from the Learn phase, prepare a realistic but challenging detailed plan for yourself to attain a Warrior Athlete level for Endurance. Your plan should cover at least the next 30 days.

Ensure that it meets PRT goals and requirements (use http://www.armyprt.com/index.shtml as reference for specific exercises).

To create this plan, identify and complete the rows for Endurance-related items on the PRT form. At least one row must be completed in the Ultimate Goals and Week sections. This plan will be the basis for tracking your progress towards achieving the Endurance badge.
